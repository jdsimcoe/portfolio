+ ABOUT ME

I am a devoted Christian that loves Jesus, my wife, life and designing for the web. This site is a snapshot of what I have done in past projects and an active reflection of what I can do, showcasing my enjoyment of making interfaces that are clean, rich, usable and highly functional.

I have a passion for design that is intuitive for users and I enjoy working with people to craft web interfaces that build and grow their business, attract new clients, and communicate effectively and clearly to their audience.

I am currently interested in working with a creative agency, preferably in a team environment, to deliver high quality client interactive experiences, while expanding my skill set. I am also available for freelance work.

+ MY PHILOSOPHY ON DESIGN

“ I believe that design is a powerful form of communication, not merely a function of aesthetics. Good design connects an audience with a message, intimating and evoking feeling, and inspiring action. That ability to shape a user’s experience pushes me to create design that has meaning and purpose. Without purpose, design is merely a replicative process that can be copied or mimicked by anyone. True design inspires, communicates clearly, and leaves something for the user to grab onto and change their own experience.”

—Jonathan Daniel Simcoe

+ RECENT WORK

 

The above video highlights a small web application that I built from scratch—an example of some recent work. Fonts are are rendered using @font-face targeting all modern browsers (including IE). CSS3 design features provide progressive enhancement and degrade gracefully for older browsers. The site takes advantage of the following technologies: ColdFusion 8, AJAX, jQuery, CSS3 (including @font-face) & HTML. One thing to note on the main screen: the Pitch Book icon was created by hand in Adobe Illustrator, my first full-fledged icon-creation effort!
